Welcome to Southern Delights

Discover the authentic taste of Southern cuisine with us. From juicy fried chicken to tender barbecue ribs, our menu is inspired by the traditional recipes passed down from generation to generation. We use only the freshest ingredients to create our delicious dishes. Our menu is sure to satisfy your cravings for Southern food.

Try Delicious Dishes

Okra

Okra is a flowering plant in the mallow family. It is valued for its edible green seed pods. The young pods and shoots are commonly used as a green vegetable in African, Asian, Caribbean, and Southern cooking.

Greens

Greens are leafy vegetables that are eaten as a vegetable or salad. They are usually served cooked, but some, such as spinach, are eaten raw. They are often served with meat or other foods.

Ham hock

Ham hock is a cut of meat from the lower part of the leg of a pig. It is a popular ingredient in Southern cooking, especially in the United States, where it is often used to make a soup called ham hock and pea soup.

Biscuits and Gravy

Biscuits and gravy is a breakfast dish consisting of biscuits and sausage gravy. It is a popular dish in the Southern United States, where it is often served with eggs and bacon.
